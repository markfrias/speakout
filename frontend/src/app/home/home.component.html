Welcome to Speakout

Speakout is a platform for AUP students to voice out their concerns about the university without the shenanigans of faking your college or batch. Start exploring articles by going to the blog or continue reading here to know more about the features of this website.

Anonymous

Create an article for submission without divulging any of your identity. Your privacy and protection is our priority

Powerful content creation tool

The blog includes a powerful text editor that includes options to include images, lists, and headers enabling everyone to draft awesome content-rich articles.

Discuss anonymously

Every article is tooled with a comments section where anyone can interact with the posts anonymously.

Fact checking and trigger warnings

Compared to other platforms where users can post anonymously, our platform includes fact-checking and trigger warnings. This ensures that the reader can be informed if the post has unverified information and the user can also be informed if there are undesirable content included.
